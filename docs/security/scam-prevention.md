# Scam Prevention

**Common crypto scams and how to protect yourself when using LalaChain.**

---

## Common Scams

### 1. Fake Support / Admin Messages

**How it works:** Someone DMs you pretending to be LalaChain support, asking for your seed phrase to "verify" your wallet or "fix" an issue.

**Reality:** No legitimate support will EVER ask for your seed phrase.

**Protection:** Block and report. Real support happens in public channels only.

---

### 2. Phishing Websites

**How it works:** A fake website that looks identical to a real one (wallet, exchange, dashboard) but captures your seed phrase or private key.

**Red flags:**
- Misspelled URLs (lalacha1n.io instead of lalachain.io)
- Asks you to "connect wallet" and then requests seed phrase
- Promoted via DMs or ads rather than official channels

**Protection:** Bookmark official URLs. Never click links in DMs.

---

### 3. Fake Airdrops / Free Token Offers

**How it works:** "Claim your free 10,000 LALA tokens! Just connect your wallet and approve this transaction."

**Reality:** The transaction drains your actual tokens.

**Protection:** Legitimate airdrops never require you to approve spending transactions.

---

### 4. Impersonation

**How it works:** Someone creates accounts mimicking team members, validators, or community leaders.

**Protection:**
- Verify through multiple channels
- Check account age and history
- Never send tokens to "verify" your account

---

### 5. Rug Pulls (DeFi/NFTs)

**How it works:** A project launches with great marketing, collects investment, then disappears with the funds.

**Red flags:**
- Anonymous team with no verifiable history
- Unrealistic returns promised (100%+ APY)
- Liquidity locked for very short periods
- No audit or fake audit

---

## General Protection Rules

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| If it's too good to be true, it is | No one gives away free money |
| Never share private keys or seed phrases | For ANY reason |
| Verify through official channels | Not DMs, not ads |
| Start small | Test with tiny amounts first |
| Use hardware wallet for large holdings | Offline = safe |
| Don't rush | Scams create artificial urgency |

---

## Reporting

If you encounter a scam:
- Report to community moderators
- Warn others in official channels
- Document the scam (screenshots) for others' awareness
- Do NOT engage with the scammer
